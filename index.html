I'm Aliona and I'm a glitterholic...


With a rich portfoliowith over 15 years of experience in the beauty industry, engaged in freelancing through providing quality services in skin care, beauty styling and body care.

Knowing how much nails can impact how we feel about presenting ourselves, motivated me to train as a nail technician so that I could help others feel able to express themselves!

My creative passion is in creating coloured acrylic designs. If you're used to having a clear acrylic with a polish on top - this is next level! You can read more about acrylics here or take a look at my social media (links at the top of the page) and see if you can spot the difference!

I pride myself on being approachable, non-judgmental and a really good listener! My salon environment where I only have one client at a time, really enables you to feel comfortable in an environment where you have my undivided attention (and unlimited tea or coffee!)

Ask yourself, who takes care of you? I have two children, so I know how hard it is to find that 'me time' without hearing "muuuuum" - having your nails done is just the bonus when you can relax, and enjoy the peace and quiet. Let me take care of you!

Whatever look you would like to achieve - I would love to hear from you. So, get yourself booked in, I'll pop the kettle on!
